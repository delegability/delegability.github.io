Contribute

Interested in how we collected this data? Disagree with some of the existing survey results and want to contribute your opinion?

We recognize our dataset is limited, and are interested in how data might change over time or for different populations. If you like, please take our survey!

We will present you with a random task. We have two versions which present different contexts for delegating the task.

  • Personal Survey:
    Choose the Personal survey to evaluate the task as if you personally are performing the task and deciding how much help you'd like from an AI.
  • Expert Survey:
    Choose the Expert survey to evaluate the task as if a human expert was performing the task on your behalf, and you need to decide how much help you're comfortable with that expert accepting from an AI.
